Introduction

Nusakambangan is the name of an island located in the southern part of the Indonesian Ocean. Sharks, known for their toughness and resilience, are commonly found in these waters. This symbolizes the strength and tenacity of the Nusakambangan soccer team, which hails from the island of Nusakambangan in the southern ocean.
