Muamalat Bank Website






Introduction

Bank Muamalat Indonesia, is the first general bank in Indonesia that applies Islamic Sharia principles in running its operations and it can be said that Bank Muamalat Indonesia is the first Islamic bank in Indonesia. Established on November 1, 1991, initiated by the Indonesian Ulema Council (MUI) and the Government of Indonesia. Started operating in 1992, supported by Muslim scholars and entrepreneurs, as well as the wider community. The increasingly developing era and the increasing growth of the younger generation in Indonesia, of course, Bank Muamalat Indonesia should update and beautify its website so that the younger generation is more interested and easier to open a new account at Bank Muamalat Indonesia and so that young customers who are numerous in Indonesia can comfortably and easily understand and get information about Bank Muamalat Indonesia.
