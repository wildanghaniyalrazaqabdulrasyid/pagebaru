Introduction

Polytron is an electronics company from Indonesia. It was founded on September 18, 1975 in Kudus, Central Java under the name PT Indonesian Electronic & Engineering, then on September 18, 1976 it changed its name to PT Hartono Istana Electronic, then in 2000 it merged and became PT Hartono Istana Teknologi. Currently, Polytron is part of the Djarum group along with BCA, Blibli, and Tiket.com .
