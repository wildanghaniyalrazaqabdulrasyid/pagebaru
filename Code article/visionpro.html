Introduction

Looking at the current new trends, it is clear that the spatial computing era is a future that will continue to grow, spatial computing has many rows and terms such as AR, VR, XR, or MR. Currently, there are many impressive spatial devices, such as Meta Quest from Meta, Apple VisionPro from Apple, Picoxr from Bytedance, Vive from HTC, and SpaceTop from Sightful. Therefore, it is appropriate for all companies and device development groups, as well as software developers, to start focusing on the development of spatial computing.
